Japanese culture is fascinating, and anime is one of the best ways to learn about. You will learn so much more than you imagine, including the folklore and myths of the country, as well as popular traditions and commonly used words into Japan.

Artisitic talents are showcased in the anime. Anime provides a massive catalog of characters because of the ability to perform in any fictional setting. Characters can vary from high school student and space cowboys to demons, samurais, ninjas, pirates, detectives, ghosts, and gods.

Not cartoon

Common misconception that anime is only for children and cartoon. This could not be further from the truth. It is suitable for all ages. While there are some anime shows that are aimed at children, there are also those that appeal to adults, as they deal with mature themes, including violence, morality, and death.
